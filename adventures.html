Golf Club

Step into a world of refined leisure at Nuwara Eliya Golf Club, one of the oldest golf clubs in Asia. Nestled amidst the breathtaking hills and rolling meadows, this beautifully maintained course offers not only exceptional golfing but also a connection to the colonial history of the region.

Waterfall Hiking

Nuwara Eliya, nestled in Sri Lanka's Central Highlands, offers enchanting waterfall hiking experiences. Among its natural wonders, Lover's Leap Waterfall stands out for its captivating beauty and ease of access, while Ramboda Falls impresses with its towering height and misty charm. These picturesque cascades beckon adventurers to explore the lush trails and revel in the breathtaking vistas they offer in this tranquil region of NuwaraEliya.
